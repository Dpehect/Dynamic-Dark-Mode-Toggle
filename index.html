Galatasaray

Galatasaray Spor Kulübü (Galatasaray SK) is Turkey's largest and most well-known football club. The club was founded in Istanbul in 1892. Galatasaray SK competes in the Turkish Super League and European cups. The club also has activities in basketball, volleyball, handball, athletics, swimming, and other sports. Galatasaray SK has achieved many successes in the field of football in Turkey and is known as "The Lions" by its fans.
